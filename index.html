WHAT WE STAND FOR
& OUR PRINCIPLES

Spark Southampton is a technology incubator program open to all students at University of Southampton. You bring your idea, we bring the help for you.

Sparks Southampton is a joint collaboration between ECS Entrepreneurs and Fish on Toast societies and we are really wanna do something awesome where everyone can benefit from joining us.

Please find some useful information below if you are interested in this amazing programme or if you already decided to want to join, just click one of the 'Apply Now' buttons!

Make a Difference

Creating something awesome that could potentially change the word.
Be the change you wish to see in the world!

Enjoy the Atmosphere

There are many like-minded people around you at SparkS, so make sure to take advantage of it and have fun together.

Real Mentors

We are here to help you all the way, so we are going to bring you in contact with mentors who can give you helpful advice in almost any field.

Find Co-founders

You have a great idea but you need help. You are the next Steve Jobs and you need your Steve Wozniak? Say no more, we can help you with that!

Practice your Skills

If you are uncomfortable to present your business proposal, we are going to hold regular workshops so you can overcome this problem.

Get Funding

You made all the necessary steps to start your own business? Go and pitch your idea to real investors at Dragons' Den and get the founding you need!

OUR SERVICES

Workshops

We are going to organize workshops specifically for your needs. They should help you on the way to progress even further.

HackSpaces

Come to our weekly program every Saturday to work on your project with equipment from Level 2 Labs. That's right, you read it correctly! Including, oscilloscopes, function generators, microcontrollers, etc. Access all the hardware you might need! We'll try to get you any hardware you might need (we just recently got an HTC Vive and a really awesome drone)!

SparkS Support

Need help in coding, in your business proposal or how to work efficiently? You can ask your questions from any of the SparkS team members and you can be sure they will help you!


Free Snacks

We are providing snacks and drinks our events, so you can really focus on what matters. Who likes to work with empty stomach...

Weekly Meetings

We want to make sure that your project is always on the right track to maximise the potential in it. We are going to sit down with you and your team and help you constantly adjust your plan!

Dragons' Den

At the end of the second semester you have the chance to be part of Dragons' Den 2017, where you can pitch to four angel investors.
